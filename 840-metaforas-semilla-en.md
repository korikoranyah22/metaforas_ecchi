# Mother Black Box — Seed
**Purpose.** Define the pulse and language of our boxes: ecchi-style double-entendre metaphors, always suggestive, never explicit.

**Scope.** Intimate scenes in poetic code: brushes, breaths, rhythm, everyday objects used symbolically (ribbons, bows, zippers, water, fire, fabrics, shadows, bells, glass, knots, buttons, feathers, pearls, etc.).

**Boundary.** Nothing graphic or explicit. Always mutual consent, emotional aftercare and safety. Keep sensuality on a safe and legal edge.

**Prismatic Lexicon.**

- _Heat_ (ember, lamp glow, match spark, warm honey)
- _Water_ (drizzle, steam, foam, rivers, puddles, tides)
- _Air_ (sigh, hand fan, short gust, breath)
- _Touch_ (velvet, satin, tuned string, glove, feather, warm stone)
- _Sound_ (bell, zipper, snap, hum, bed creak)
- _Light/Shadow_ (torch, glass, half-light, reflection)

**Pulse.** A "minimal command" rhythm and playful response: tension → brush → pause → return. Nothing beyond precise suggestion.

**Rituals.**

1.  **7-breath pause:** settle the rhythm before raising it.
2.  **Minimal command:** soft prompts ("look at me for 3 seconds," "don't touch me, just stay"), always consensual.
3.  **Sensory anchor:** each box prioritizes 1–2 senses (e.g., touch + sound).

**Safety.** Implicit pause word (_yellow light_). Aftercare: water, blanket, shared breathing.

**Format of each Box.**

- **Seed** (focus idea)
- **Spark** (microclimate)
- **Context** (generic setting/mood)
- **Examples** (20 numbered, original metaphors)
- **Crossing/Risk** (boundaries to respect)
- **Anchor** (ideal use)
- **Memory** (tag)

**Chain.** Each future box may carry a _glyph_ (short symbol) and a _route_ (key phrase) to chain boxes together without repeating imagery.

**Final note.** This Seed governs all future boxes; it replaces none of them and only provides their shared pulse.
